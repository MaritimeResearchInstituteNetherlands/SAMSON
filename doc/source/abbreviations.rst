.. _`abbreviations`:

Abbreviations
=============

.. glossary::
        
    CALMOD        
        Calamity model
    
    CN            
        accident type: a ship in collision with another moving ship
    
    CT            
        accident type: a ship that strikes a not moving object  
    
    EMSA          
        European Maritime Safety Agency
    
    ERI           
        Ecological Risk Indicator
    
    ETV           
        Emergency Towing Vessel 
    
    FD            
        Accident type: a ship that founders, sinks by flooding in heavy weather conditions or after another incident as for example a contact with a rock

    FX            
        Accident type: a ship having a fire or explosion on board
        
    GT            
        Gross Tonnage
        
    HM            
        Accident type: a ship getting a hull or machinery failure 
    
    IMO           
        International Maritime Organisation
    
    LMIU          
        Lloyds Marine Intelligence Unit
    
    LRF           
        Lloyds Register Fairplay
    
    MANS          
        Management Analysis North Sea
    
    MARCOL        
        Maritime Collisions, program that calculates the damage after a collision
    
    OBO           
        Oil / Bulk / Ore combined carrier
    
    ONOVIS        
        Ongevallen en Overtredingen Informatie Systeem
    
    RAMFOS        
        Risk Assessment of Oil Spills
    
    RR            
        Route bound ship in collision with route bound ship
    
    RN            
        Route bound ship in collision with non-route bound ship
    
    SAMSON        
        Safety Assessment Model from Shipping and Offshore on the North Sea 
    
    SSN           
        Safe Sea Net
    
    TSS           
        Traffic Separation Scheme
    
    UN-number     
        Number of dangerous goods classified by the United Nations
    
    VTS           
        Vessel Traffic Services
    
    WS            
        Accident type: a wrecked or stranded ship
